File a Case

How to File Your Case with ICAMEK

Follow these steps to commence arbitration or mediation proceedings under the ICAMEK Rules.

01
Download the Request Form

Download the appropriate form: Request for Arbitration or Request for Mediation from the ICAMEK website.

02
Pay the Registration Fee

Pay the registration fee of UGX 350,000/= and obtain proof of payment to submit with your request.

03
Submit Your Request

Email your completed form and proof of payment to file@icamek.org, or deliver in person to 4th Floor, Trust Tower, Nakasero Road.

04
ICAMEK Confirms & Proceeds

ICAMEK confirms receipt and notifies the Respondent. We then guide all parties through the process to resolution.

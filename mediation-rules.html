ICAMEK Mediation Rules

The Governing Rules for All Mediation Proceedings at ICAMEK

Governing Rules

ICAMEK Mediation Rules 2018

ICAMEK has published a set of Mediation Rules which apply in the event of a dispute referred to ICAMEK for mediation. These rules provide the procedural framework within which all mediations are conducted.

ICAMEK offers a confidential mediation process where an independent mediator facilitates communication and open discussion to assist the parties in reaching a settlement in private. ICAMEK's mediation focuses primarily on the needs and interests of the parties.

Depending on the nature of the dispute, ICAMEK will appoint a suitable mediator and professionally administer the process. Ground rules are established at the outset and the mediator may engage all parties together or privately to find common ground.

Mediation is not a binding process, but once consensus is reached, a written agreement is drawn up which commits the parties to their agreement and is legally binding.

Key Highlights of the ICAMEK Mediation Rules
Confidential Process

ICAMEK offers a confidential mediation process where an independent mediator facilitates communication and open discussion to assist the parties in reaching a settlement in private.

Mediator Appointment

Depending on the nature of the dispute, ICAMEK will appoint a suitable mediator and professionally administer the process. The mediator is independent and impartial.

Parties' Interests First

ICAMEK's mediation focuses primarily on the needs and interests of the parties  an interactive process providing expertise and support to reach agreement, whether to preserve relationships or resolve a single dispute.

Flexible Process

Ground rules are established at the outset and the mediator may engage all parties together or privately to find common ground. This flexibility allows for creative and practical solutions.

Legally Binding Agreement

Mediation is not a binding process, but once consensus is reached, a written agreement is drawn up which commits the parties to their agreement and is legally binding.

Faster than Litigation

Mediation is significantly faster and less costly than court litigation, allowing parties to reach resolution and move forward with their commercial relationships intact.

ICAMEK Mediation Model Clause

"If any dispute or difference of any kind whatsoever shall arise between the parties in connection with or arising out of this agreement, the parties shall first seek to resolve any such dispute or difference by mediation in accordance with the Mediation Rules of the International Centre for Arbitration and Mediation in Kampala (ICAMEK)."

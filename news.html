October 2024

Construction Claims & Dispute Avoidance Training Programme

ICAMEK offers a specialised training programme on Construction Claims & Dispute Avoidance, facilitated by Mrs. Olivia Kyarimpa Matovu MCIArb, Partner at Ligomarc Advocates. The programme is designed for construction professionals including lawyers, engineers, architects, quantity surveyors and project managers. Venue: Latitude 0 Hotel, Mobutu Road, Makindye, Kampala.

Announcement
April 2019

ICAMEK Officially Launched by Deputy Chief Justice Richard Buteera

The International Centre for Arbitration and Mediation in Kampala (ICAMEK) was officially launched at Trust Tower, Nakasero on 25 April 2019. The ceremony was presided over by Deputy Chief Justice Richard Buteera, with Hon. Kahinda Otafiire, Minister of Justice and Constitutional Affairs, in attendance. ICAMEK received its appointing authority instrument from the Minister on 23 April 2019.

Event
Annual Event

ICAMEK University Moot Competition  Annual ADR Challenge

ICAMEK runs an annual University Moot Competition providing law students across Uganda with practical experience in arbitration and dispute resolution. The competition exposes students to the world of commercial arbitration, helping to build the next generation of ADR practitioners in Uganda. Participating universities engage in realistic simulation exercises judged by senior practitioners.

ADR Update
2024

ICAMEK Arbitration & Mediation Rules 2018  Now Available for Download

ICAMEK's published Arbitration and Mediation Rules are now available for free download on the ICAMEK website. The rules provide the procedural framework for all ICAMEK-administered proceedings. Parties wishing to include ICAMEK arbitration or mediation clauses in their agreements can also download the model clauses from the ICAMEK website.

Partnerships
2019 Present

ICAMEK Partners with Uganda's Leading Private Sector Institutions

ICAMEK was established through the collaborative effort of Uganda's leading legal, financial, and private sector organisations including the Uganda Law Society, Uganda Bankers' Association, Private Sector Foundation Uganda (PSFU), Uganda Insurers Association, and more. These partners share ICAMEK's commitment to advancing the ADR ecosystem in Uganda and across East Africa.

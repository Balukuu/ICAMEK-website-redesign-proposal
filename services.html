How to File a Case

01
Submit the Request

The request is submitted by the Claimant to ICAMEK via email (file@icamek.org) or physically at 4th Floor Trust Tower, together with proof of payment of UGX 350,000.

02
ICAMEK Notifies Respondent

ICAMEK writes to the Respondent to notify them of the request for arbitration.

03
Advance on Costs

Based on the parties' claims, ICAMEK calculates the advance on costs paid by the parties in equal shares unless otherwise stated in the arbitration agreement.

04
Case Transmitted

When advances are paid and the arbitral tribunal is constituted, ICAMEK refers the case to the arbitral tribunal for them to continue the proceedings.

05
Hearings & Award

A hearing is held as appropriate. The Arbitral Tribunal renders the final award, which is final and binding on the parties when rendered.

02
Mediation

Confidential Mediation

ICAMEK offers a confidential mediation process where an independent mediator facilitates communication and open discussion to assist the parties in reaching a settlement in private.

ICAMEK's mediation focuses primarily on the needs and interests of the parties and is an interactive process whereby the mediator provides expertise and support to parties who wish to reach agreement, whether to preserve their relationship or to resolve a single dispute.

Ground rules are established at the outset and the mediator may engage all parties together or privately to find common ground. ICAMEK has published a set of Mediation Rules which apply in the event of a dispute referred to ICAMEK for mediation.

Depending on the nature of the dispute, ICAMEK will appoint a suitable mediator and professionally administer the process. Mediation is not a binding process, but once consensus is reached, a written agreement is drawn up which commits the parties to their agreement and is legally binding.

Conciliation

Conciliation Services

ICAMEK also provides administrative support in other forms of alternative dispute resolution, including mediation and conciliation. Conciliation is similar to mediation but the conciliator generally takes a more proactive role in proposing terms of settlement.

06
Secretariat

Secretariat Services

The Centre's secretariat is tasked with administering the Mediation and Arbitration process by providing clear procedural guidelines with time-lines, and a case administrator to assist the tribunal in the collation of documents and assist parties in complying with the Mediator/tribunal's directions.

A dedicated case administrator is assigned to each case, providing expert support throughout the process and ensuring smooth and timely proceedings in accordance with the applicable ICAMEK Rules.

ADR Training Programs

Growing Uganda's Pool of ADR Practitioners

Our Programs

Professional ADR Training & Accreditation

The Centre offers training programs across the spectrum of dispute resolution (mediation, arbitration, construction claim), geared towards growing the pool of ADR practitioners in Uganda. The courses are presented by seasoned practitioners in the different fields and enables participants to master essential skills in all aspects of dispute resolution which will be of enormous importance to their careers and their clients.

ICAMEK offers Ugandan companies and individuals an optimal process to resolve business and related disputes confidentially, quickly and fairly. Our training programs prepare lawyers, engineers, accountants, and other professionals to play a meaningful role in this ecosystem as certified ADR practitioners.

Specialist Course
Construction Claims & Dispute Avoidance

A specialised training programme focused on the identification, valuation, negotiation and avoidance of construction claims and disputes. This course equips construction professionals, lawyers, engineers and project managers with practical tools to prevent and resolve disputes in the construction sector.

Venue: Latitude 0 Hotel, Mobutu Road, Makindye, Kampala
Target Audience: Lawyers, engineers, architects, quantity surveyors, project managers, and construction professionals

Moot Competition

ICAMEK University Moot Competition

ICAMEK runs an annual University Moot Competition, providing law students across Uganda with practical experience in arbitration and dispute resolution. The competition exposes students to the world of commercial arbitration, helping to build the next generation of ADR practitioners in Uganda.

Participating universities engage in realistic arbitration simulations, with student teams acting as counsel for claimants and respondents before a panel of experienced arbitrators. The competition is judged by senior practitioners drawn from ICAMEK's panel.

The competition serves ICAMEK's broader mission of promoting commercial awareness of arbitration and ADR mechanisms through advocacy and education.

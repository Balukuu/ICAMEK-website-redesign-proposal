Who We Are

Uganda's Premier Centre for Alternative Dispute Resolution

ICAMEK is an independent, not-for-profit organisation, dedicated to advancing Alternative Dispute Resolution in Uganda and across East Africa.

ICAMEK is the first private sector-led institution focused on delivering the benefits of world-class Alternative Dispute Resolution to businesses, professionals, governments and communities alike.

We provide dispute resolution services to Ugandan parties and the international business communities who wish to have their disputes resolved outside of the public courtroom.

Why Choose ADR

Advantages of Arbitration Over Court Litigation

Expert Arbitrators
Unlike in court, parties can select an arbitrator with an appropriate degree of practical experience relevant to their dispute.
Faster Resolution
Arbitration is often faster than litigation in court, and a time limit can be placed on the length of the process.
International Enforceability
Due to the provisions of the New York Convention 1958, arbitral awards are generally easier to enforce in other nations than court judgments.
Limited Appeals
Unlike in court, there are very limited avenues for appeal of an arbitral award, which limits the duration of the dispute and any associated liability.
Confidential Proceedings
Unlike court rulings, arbitration proceedings and arbitral awards are confidential and not on public record.
Flexible Procedures
Procedures can be designed by the parties and the tribunal, making the process more commercial and less formal than court.
Neutral Venue
Parties can ensure that their disputes are resolved at an independent neutral venue and seat, free from the influence of any single party's home jurisdiction.

The Process

How ICAMEK Resolves Your Dispute

01
Submit Request

The request for arbitration is submitted by the Claimant to ICAMEK via email (file@icamek.org) or physically at 4th Floor Trust Tower, together with proof of payment.

02
Registration & Fee

Under the ICAMEK Rules the registration fee amounts to UGX 350,000/=. ICAMEK calculates the advance on costs paid by the parties in equal shares.

03
Tribunal Constituted

When the advances are paid and the arbitral tribunal is constituted, ICAMEK refers the case to the arbitral tribunal to continue the proceedings.

04
Hearings

A hearing shall be held if requested by a party, or if deemed appropriate by the Arbitral Tribunal. The Tribunal shall determine the date, time and location.

05
Award Rendered

The Arbitral Tribunal renders the final award. An award is final and binding on the parties when rendered, enforceable under the New York Convention.
